NBSA-boundary chart

Legend:

  • Green: All obtainable credits earned.
  • Yellow: Passed the NBSA boundary of 45.
  • Red: Not passed the NBSA boundary of 45.

The NBSA boundary shows the amount of EC's / study credits needed to pass the first year. This boundary is at 45 EC's, the total amount of EC's you can earn is 60. If you don't reach the boundary at the end of the study year, you can't continue with the program. If you reach between 45 and 60 you pass to the second year. Finally, if you get all 60 EC's then you get your propedeuse.
